Formative Q&A

Here you can find answer to our most asked questions

  • How can I create a form?
  • You can create form from dashboard when clicking the "Create New" button, or when clicking the "New Form" button from sidebar located on your left side of screen.
  • I can't see all my forms, where are there?
  • By clicking "Show more" on dashboard, you will see more of your forms.
  • How can I rename a form?
  • When in a form editor, by clicking the form name located on top of the screen, you will enter to a name editing mode and will be able to change form name.
  • I want to delete one of my questions. Where can I do that?
  • When in a form editor, by clicking the trash bin located on right of the question, you will delete the question with all of its answers from a form.
  • I want to delete a answer. How can I do that?
  • When in a form editor, by clicking the cross button located on right of the answer, you will delete the answer from a question.
  • Where can I publish my form?
  • You can publish and manage invites to your form by clicking the globe button when in dashboard. This wil redirect you to a new page where you can create a link to the form.
  • Can I change my account details?
  • You can change all your account details from "User update" page. This page is accesible by clicking the user icon on the top right side of your page and choosing the "User settings" icon.
  • Is it possible to change my user icon?
  • Yes. You can chage it from "User update" page by using "Profile picture" setting. This page is accesible by clicking the user icon on the top right side of your page and choosing the "User settings" icon.

Thank you for using Formative!
